NYC Class-4 Commercial Assessment Screen

This tool screens a New York City commercial property's tax assessment against comparable properties. Instead of manually pulling and comparing twenty or more assessment comps to sanity-check a tax basis, an underwriter enters an address or BBL and sees where the subject sits — estimated market value, tax bill, and value per square foot — within a distribution of nearby peers. Comparables are selected deterministically by measured property type, building size, and location; every figure traces to a public source — the NYC Department of Finance assessment roll (8y4t-faws) and PLUTO — with no invented numbers and no verdicts. It returns a distribution to read, not a conclusion.
